# Taxes

The Taxes page provides monthly tax summaries, detailed VAT reports, and access to previously filed tax records for compliance and financial management.

---

## 1. Page Overview
When you open the Taxes page, the following option is available at the top-right corner:

### 1.1 Sender Details Button
The Sender Details button allows you to configure or view sender information required for official tax reporting and documentation purposes.

---

## 2. Monthly Tax Reports
Below the top section, monthly tax report cards such as February Month Report and January Month Report are displayed.

Each monthly report card provides a financial summary including:

- Gross Amount
- Net Amount
- Taxable Amount

Each report card contains a top-right arrow icon. Clicking this arrow opens the detailed tax report page for the selected month.

---

## Detailed Monthly Tax Report Page
When you click the arrow on a specific month report, a detailed tax report page opens.

At the top of this page:

- A back arrow is available on the left side to navigate back to the Taxes page.
- The selected month report title is displayed clearly.
- On the right side, Select Location and Calendar options allow you to filter the report based on location and custom date ranges.

Below the header section:

Four summary cards are displayed showing Gross Amount, Net Amount, Transactions, and Taxable Amount.

On the right side, a Revenue Breakdown pie chart displays payment distribution such as Credit/Debit Card and Membership Card.

Further below, an Export button is available to download the tax report.

The Tax Details table displays structured information including Machine No., Date, City, Location, Items Sold, Gross, Net, and VAT Amount (19%).

---

## 3. Previous Records
Below the Monthly Tax Reports section, the Previous Records section is displayed.

At the top of this section, a Search option is available to quickly locate specific records.

The Previous Records table includes the following columns: Month, Status, Filed Date, Gross Amount, Net Amount, Taxable Amount, and Due Amount.

This section helps you track previously filed tax reports, review status updates, and monitor outstanding due amounts.
