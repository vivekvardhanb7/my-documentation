
# Help

The Help page provides quick access to support resources, documentation, and important legal information.

---

## Support Options
Below the introduction section, three support cards are displayed:

- Documentation
- FAQs
- Trust & Safety / Imprint

---

## Documentation
When you click the top-right arrow on the Documentation card, the full system guide page opens.

This page includes:

### Back Button
Return to the Help page anytime.

### Intro
Overview of the NAF Cloud System and its purpose.

### Home
View daily sales, machine status, and key performance indicators.

### View
Analyze sales, transactions, billing, and payment trends with charts.

### Manage
Manage customers, discounts, coupons, inventory, advertisements, and users.

### Taxes
Access automatically generated monthly tax reports and export them.

### Maintenance
Submit service requests and track their status.

### Franchise
Manage franchise details and associated vending machines.

This section provides a complete step-by-step guide to using the system efficiently.

---

## FAQs
When you click the top-right arrow on the FAQs card, the Frequently Asked Questions page opens.

It includes:

- Login & password help
- Dashboard navigation guidance
- Data export instructions
- Tax report information
- Maintenance request process
- Technical support details

This section helps users quickly find answers without contacting support.

---

## Trust & Safety / Imprint
When you click the top-right arrow on the Trust & Safety card, the legal information page opens.

It includes:

- Company details
- Legal disclosures
- Privacy policy
- Terms & conditions
- Contact information

This ensures transparency and compliance.
