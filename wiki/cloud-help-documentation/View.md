# View – Sales Analytics

The Sales Analytics page provides a complete overview of your sales performance, location insights, payment analysis, and product trends in one centralized dashboard.

---

## 1. Sales Summary
At the top of the page, you will see four sales summary cards that provide a quick snapshot of business performance.

### 1.1 Today’s Sales
Displays the total revenue generated for the current day.

### 1.2 This Week’s Sales
Shows the total sales for the ongoing week.

### 1.3 This Month’s Sales
Represents the total sales accumulated during the current month.

### 1.4 This Year’s Sales
Displays the overall sales performance for the current year.

These summary cards allow you to quickly monitor business performance across different time periods.

---

## 2. Total Sales Graph
The Total Sales Graph visually represents overall revenue trends.

### 2.1 Time Filter Options
In the top-right corner, you can select Day, Week, Month, or Year to adjust the graph view based on the selected time period.

This feature helps you analyze short-term and long-term sales performance easily.

---

## 3. Sales by Locations
The Sales by Locations section displays sales distribution across different machine locations.

### 3.1 Date Filter
A calendar icon is available in the top-right corner. When clicked, it allows you to select Daily or Continuous view, choose specific calendar dates, and click Apply to update the graph.

This helps in analyzing location-based sales for custom date ranges.

---

## 4. Location Selection & Sales Breakdown
This section allows you to filter sales data and view payment and discount insights.

### 4.1 Select Location
Clicking the Select Location button opens a search field with available location names, where you can select one or multiple locations and click Save to apply the filter.

### 4.2 Payment Methods
This pie chart displays sales distribution by Debit Card, Credit Card, and Membership Card.

This helps you understand which payment method is most commonly used.

### 4.3 Sales with Coupons & Discounts
This pie chart shows the breakdown of Net Total, Coupons, Discount, and Total values.

This section helps you understand customer payment preferences and the financial impact of coupons and discounts.

---

## 5. Top Selling Products & Data Export
This final section highlights product performance and reporting options.

### 5.1 Top Selling Products
Displays a list of best-performing products based on sales volume and revenue.

### 5.2 Download Option
Allows you to export sales data for reporting, record keeping, and business analysis purposes.

---

# Transactions

The Transactions page provides a complete overview of all machine transactions. It allows you to monitor transaction performance, apply filters, review transaction history, and export reports when needed.

---

## 1. Transaction Summary
At the top of the page, three summary cards provide a quick overview of transaction performance.

### 1.1 Total Transactions
Displays the total number of transactions recorded for the selected period.

### 1.2 Successful Transactions
Shows the number of successfully completed transactions.

### 1.3 Unsuccessful Transactions
Displays the number of failed or incomplete transactions.

These summary cards help you quickly understand the overall transaction success rate.

---

## 2. Filters, Location Selection & Transaction History
This section allows you to filter transactions, analyze records, and export data.

### 2.1 Select Location
Clicking the Select Location button opens a search field with available location names, where you can select one or multiple locations and click Save to apply the filter.

### 2.2 Date Filter
A calendar icon is available to select Daily or Continuous view, choose specific dates from the calendar, and click Apply to update the transaction data.

### 2.3 Transaction Status Filter
On the right side, the Filter button provides a dropdown with the options All, Completed, and Failed, allowing you to filter transactions based on their status.

### 2.4 Export Option
Next to the Filter button, the Export option allows you to download transaction data for reporting and record-keeping purposes.

### 2.5 Transaction History
Below the filters, the Transaction History table displays detailed records including Date, Transaction ID, Location, Machine, Number of Products, Offer Total, Payment Type, Status, and Reason.
